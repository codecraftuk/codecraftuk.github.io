We are a software crafters meetup. We discuss, demonstrate and debate how to write well-structured, malleable software

We meet on the 3rd Thursday of every month

We are a nomadic meetup: the location (within Glasgow) changes every month. We try to run in office spaces around the city, but occasionally meet in a pub; there is no expectation or pressure to drink alcohol when we do so.
